Xiangye Lin

I am Xiangye Lin, a first-year Master student in Computer Science at UMass Amherst, advised by Prof. Chuang Gan. Prior to that, I received my bachelor’s degree from Zhejiang University, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Lanfen Lin during my undergraduate.

My research interest lies in Embodied AI and Agents. I’m deeply interested in building embodied multi-agent systems that operate at real-world scale.

Outside of research, I enjoy bird watching and nature photography.
